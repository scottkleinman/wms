Friday 22 March 2013 13.19 EDT	Informa	informa.com	Informa is a multinational publisher and conference company, serving academics, and professional and commercial businesses. Its academic division publishes more than 55,000 titles – mainly in the humanities, social sciences and science and technology – while its professional and commercial division provides businesses with databases, subscription-based services, real-time news and research. Its events and training division, meanwhile, runs major exhibitions, trade conferences, seminars and training events across various business sectors. In fact, Informa is the world's largest publicly owned organiser of exhibitions, events and training. As an employer, Informa offers structured training, tailored to individual development needs, and – with 150 offices in 40 countries – it also offers opportunities for career movement.
